Cloudy Hotel

Where Cloudy’s famous harbour meets the Pacific Ocean amid gleaming panoramas and grand headlands, Cloudy Hotel nestles on the sandy shoreline. A destination in itself, our exclusive enclave captures the feel of a secret seaside haven so completely, you’d never guess how close you are to Cloudy’s busy heart. Settle in, slow down and savour the simple things: a long lunch at Beach Club overlooking the boats and blue vistas; a barefoot stroll on the sand; sipping a sundowner while the big star paints the sky in dreamy pastels as it slips behind the horizon.
